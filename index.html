Panic! at the Disco

Who is Panic! at the Disco?

Panic! at the Disco is a pop/rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada that as formed in 2004. The band was formed by childhood friends Brendon Urie, Ryan Ross, Spencer Smith, and Brent Wilson. Since their formation all the orignal members except for Brendon Urie have left the band. They have released six studio albums and various other band members have come and gone. To this day Brendon Urie remains writing and releasing songs with new band memebers Kenneth Harris, Dan Pawlovich, and Nicole Row.
